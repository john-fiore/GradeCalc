About Me

I'm John, and I created GradeCalc. I created this website because I wanted to help other fellow high school students figure out what their exact grade is - without having to bust out a calculator.

I hope this website helps you in some way!

A bit more about me

I'm 17 years old and I'm from the Chicago suburbs. Outside of school, I like to develop apps, websites, and games. At the moment, besides developing this, I have been developing a horror game for the past year. All I will say about it now, is that it is atmospherically inspired by games like Amnesia: The Dark Descent and the Half-Life horror mod Cry of Fear.
